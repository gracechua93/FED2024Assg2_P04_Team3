FAQ

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Credit/Debit Cards, PayPal, and Digital Wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

How long does it take to get a response?

Our support team typically responds within 24-48 hours.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us through our Contact Page or email us directly at support@mokesell.com.

How do I apply a bump for my listings?

Click on your account at the top of the page. Select the seller option and it will bring you to the seller page. Click on the Promote header on the left and you will be able to apply it there.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your information is only used to resolve your feedback and will not be shared with third parties.

Why am I unable to log in to my account?

Ensure your email and password are entered correctly.
Check if your account is activated (verify your email).
